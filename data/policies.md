# Northwind Outdoors — Customer Policies

These are the official store policies used by the support team. Each section is a self-contained
policy document. The support copilot retrieves the relevant section and cites it by heading.

## Shipping

We ship to the United States and Canada. Standard shipping is **free on orders over $75**;
orders under $75 are charged a flat **$6.95**. Standard delivery takes **3–7 business days**.
Expedited 2-day shipping is available for **$19.95**. Orders placed before **1:00 PM ET on a
business day** ship the same day; orders after that cutoff, or on weekends/holidays, ship the next
business day. We do not ship to PO boxes for expedited orders.

## Returns

Unused items in original condition with tags attached may be returned within **30 days of
delivery** for a full refund to the original payment method. Items must be returned with proof of
purchase (order number). **Worn, washed, or damaged items are not eligible** for a standard refund.
Return shipping is **free** when the return is caused by our error (wrong or defective item);
otherwise a **$7.95 return-shipping fee** is deducted from the refund. Refunds are issued within
**5 business days** of us receiving the item.

## Final Sale

Items marked **Final Sale**, gift cards, and used personal-hygiene items (e.g., hydration
mouthpieces) are **not returnable or refundable** under any circumstances. Final Sale items do not
carry the standard 30-day return window. Warranty coverage for manufacturing defects still applies
where a warranty is offered.

## Warranty

Products carry a manufacturer warranty against defects in materials and workmanship for the period
listed on the product page (typically **1–3 years**). Warranty covers defects, **not** normal wear,
misuse, or accidental damage. For a valid warranty claim we replace or repair the item at no cost,
including shipping both ways. Warranty claims require the order number and a photo of the defect.
Warranty does not cover items past their listed warranty period.

## Price Match

We match the current advertised price of an identical, in-stock item from an authorized retailer
within **14 days of your purchase**. Submit the competitor link with your order number and we refund
the difference. Price match excludes clearance, marketplace third-party sellers, and bundle
promotions.

## Order Changes and Cancellations

Orders can be changed or cancelled **before they ship**. Once an order has shipped it cannot be
cancelled, but it can be returned under the Returns policy. To request a change or cancellation,
contact support with your order number as soon as possible.
